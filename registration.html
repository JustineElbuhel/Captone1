    Phy·to·phile

    /faɪˈtəʊˈfaɪl/

    Phyto comes from the Greek 'phytón' meaning “plant”.
    Phile comes from Greek 'philos' meaning “dear, beloved”.
    Someone with a love or affinity for plants.
